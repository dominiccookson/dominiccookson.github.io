Contact

If you think that counselling could help you, then you can contact me to book a first session on:

info@dominiccookson.co.uk
07941 864 762

The first session is for you to outline the reasons you are seeking counselling. It is also a chance for us to decide whether I can offer what you are looking for, and whether you feel that I would be the right counsellor for you. If you decide to proceed we will then confirm a regular weekly slot for your counselling.
