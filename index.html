Space to talk, think and grow


Welcome to Dominic Cookson Counselling. If you are finding life difficult at the moment, counselling can help you to find a way forward.


I provide understanding and supportive confidential therapy in Waterloo, Shoreditch and Canary Wharf. If you feel that talking to someone might help, you can contact me to book an introductory counselling session, or simply to ask questions and find out more.
